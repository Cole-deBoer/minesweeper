Rules:

Within this game the player will first select a cell on the baord. In this variannt the players first cell that the player selects will not contain a mine, only after the selection will the rest of the board be populated. Throughout the game the player must use information given from the visible cells to determine which cells are safe to open, and which ones contain mines. upon selecting a non-mine cell, the player will be given information on how many mines are within the eight sorrounding cells, This information is presented in the form of a number from 1-8. The player must place flags down on the cells that they believe contain mines, and they must uncover every cell that doesnt contain a mine. Upon successful completion of this task the player will win the game.
